Ear

The human ear is a remarkable organ responsible for both hearing and balance. It consists of three main anatomical divisions: the outer ear, middle ear, and inner ear, each playing a crucial role in sound perception and equilibrium.

Eye

The human eye is a sophisticated optical instrument capable of detecting light and converting it into electrochemical signals that the brain processes into visual images.

Mouth

The mouth plays essential roles in speaking and breathing, containing teeth, tongue, salivary glands, and various muscles that work together to perform these functions.
